WHY THE OBE Collective?

Owing to Covid, the university exams in 2020 will be virtual and open-book. The grades will be largely dictated by how accessible quality resources are to us. This initiative is an attempt to create a bridge to ensure there’s a level-playing field for everyone. Now, more than ever, it is important that no one is left behind only because of financial constraints. We want to ensure that every student can access good-quality material for it shouldn’t be a privilege but a fundamental right.
